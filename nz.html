New Zealand

Why It’s Special: New Zealand is special because of its beautiful nature, friendly people, and outdoor adventures. It has everything from tall mountains and clear lakes to green hills and sandy beaches. You can visit places like Milford Sound, Hobbiton, and Rotorua to see amazing landscapes and learn about Māori culture. It is a peaceful and safe country, perfect for travelers who love nature.

How to Get There: Fly from the Philippines to Auckland, Wellington, or Christchurch.

Other Places to Visit: Queenstown, Rotorua, Milford Sound, Wellington.
