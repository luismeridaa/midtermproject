Italy

Why It’s Special: Italy is special because of its history, art, and delicious food. It has famous landmarks like the Colosseum, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and Venice’s canals. The country is also known for pizza, pasta, and gelato. Walking through Italian streets feels like stepping back in time, with old buildings and beautiful architecture everywhere.

How to Get There: Fly from the Philippines to Rome, Milan, or Venice.

Other Places to Visit: Rome, Venice, Florence, Amalfi Coast.
