My Achievements

Graduated Senior High School with Honors

Graduating with honors in senior high school was one of my proudest moments. It was the result of my hard work, late-night studying, and dedication to my goals. Even when my subjects were difficult, I always did my best. Balancing school and other activities was not easy, but I kept going because I knew my efforts would be worth it in the end. Getting honors was not just about good grades—it also taught me important lessons like time management, discipline, and patience. I learned how to handle stress, stay focused, and keep trying even when things were tough. My family, teachers, and friends supported me along the way, and I am very thankful for their encouragement.

My first solo overseas trip

My first solo overseas trip was an unforgettable experience. This trip is my biggest achievement this year. At first, I felt both excited and nervous because it was my first time traveling alone to a new country. I had to go to the U.S. to babysit my sister's dog because she and her husband were on a one-month trip to Mexico. This trip was a big step for me, but I was eager to explore a new place and experience a different culture. Traveling alone taught me many important lessons. I became more independent and confident in making decisions. I also improved my problem-solving skills, especially when I had to navigate unfamiliar streets or communicate with people who spoke a different language. Even though I faced some challenges, like getting lost or feeling homesick, I learned to stay calm and find solutions on my own. This trip made me realize how exciting and rewarding solo travel can be. It gave me a sense of freedom and adventure that I will always cherish. After this experience, I feel more prepared and excited to explore more places in the future. Traveling alone showed me that I am capable of handling new challenges and making the most of every journey.

Travel the world

Traveling the world is one of my biggest dreams. I love the idea of visiting new places, learning about different cultures, and meeting people from around the world. I want to see famous landmarks, try different kinds of food, and experience the traditions of other countries. For me, traveling is not just about taking pictures—it's about making memories and learning new things. One of the best things about traveling is the adventure it brings. Going to a new place helps me step out of my comfort zone and become more independent. I know that traveling will teach me important lessons, like how to find my way in a new city and understand different ways of life. But what makes traveling even more special is doing it with my best friends and family. Exploring new places together, sharing fun moments, and trying new things make every trip even more exciting. Whether it’s getting lost, laughing over new foods, or simply enjoying the view, these memories are even better when shared with the people I love.
